Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my proposal limit?

You'll receive a notification when you're close to your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional proposals as needed.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may have implementation costs depending on your requirements.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. Free trial users can cancel at any time.
